(1) Select a User ID to pull up your gym routine.
(2) Navigate through the calendar. When you select a day in a different month, the pie chart will update to that month.
(3) Click "View & Edit Routine" to edit your gym routine for that selected day.
(4) Use buttons in the command frame to make changes to your routine, which will be save as you go (Cmd and Shift to select multiple records).
(5) Click "Autogenerate Task" to get a recommendation to even out your monthly muscle distribution or get an exercise in a specific category.
